What is #Grandma?

We send your instagram photos to your loved ones who don't use social networking. They are automatically sent via email or snail mail without you having to do anything.

What if I don't want my Grandma to see all of my photos?

Just tag the photos that you do want to send with #grandma or another keyword of your choice.
